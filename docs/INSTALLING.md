# Installing ShortPath

## Windows

1. Download `ShortPath-Setup-x.x.x.exe` from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Dadpops/ShortPath/releases).
2. Double-click the installer. Windows may show a blue notice: **"Windows protected your PC."**
   This is expected for a newly published app. Click **More info**, then **Run anyway**.
3. Follow the installer prompts. ShortPath does not require administrator rights by default.
4. After install, ShortPath starts automatically and appears in the system tray (bottom-right corner of the taskbar).

Your data is stored in `%APPDATA%\ShortPath` — outside the app bundle — so reinstalling or updating never affects your saved entries or settings.

## Mac

1. Download `ShortPath-x.x.x.dmg` from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Dadpops/ShortPath/releases).
2. Open the DMG and drag ShortPath into your Applications folder.
3. On first launch, macOS may show: **"ShortPath cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified."**
   This is expected. To open it:
   - Right-click (or Control-click) the ShortPath icon and choose **Open**, then click **Open** in the dialog.
   - Or go to **System Settings > Privacy & Security**, scroll down, and click **Open Anyway** next to the ShortPath notice.
4. ShortPath starts and appears in the menu bar.

Your data is stored in `~/Library/Application Support/ShortPath` — outside the app bundle — so reinstalling or updating never affects your saved entries or settings.
